About CUNYHousing

Built by CUNY students, for CUNY students.

Our Mission

The problem our team is tackling is helping CUNY students find affordable housing, as New York City rents are among the highest in the country and most campuses offer little to no on-campus housing. This forces students into an unstable situation that threatens their ability to stay enrolled and succeed academically.

What We Built

CUNYHousing is a free website that helps CUNY students search for NYCHA affordable housing developments near their campus. Students can filter by campus, borough, and price range, and are directed to NYC Housing Connect to check for open applications.
